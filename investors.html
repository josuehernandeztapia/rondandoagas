Frictionless Payment Ecosystem

We designed a collection system that adapts to the operator's real cash flow, not the other way around. This drastically reduces friction and the likelihood of non-payment.

A portion of the payment is covered by the real savings generated from NGV consumption. The unit partly pays for itself while it works.

2. Flexible Remainder Payment

Key Tech: Integration with payment gateways (STP, OXXO Pay) for automatic reconciliation.
